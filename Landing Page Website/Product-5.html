Our Product

Custom balisong

These are balisong knives that are designed and made by individual knife makers, each of them can have different designs, materials and features.

Filipino-style balisong

This type of balisong is made in the Philippines and features a traditional design with carved or etched handles and a blade made of high-carbon steel.

Benchmade balisong

This type of balisong is made by the popular knife manufacturer Benchmade and features a more modern design with precision-machined titanium or stainless steel handles and a high-quality blade.

About Us

We introduce authentic products from Batangas city including Kapeng Barako, Lomi, and Atchara. Our website provides detailed information about these traditional products, bringing the rich flavors and heritage of Batangas directly to you.
